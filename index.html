About PROCOMM

IEEE Communication Project Competition, PROCOMM is the flagship event of the IEEE ComSoc Kerala Chapter with participation from students all over Kerala. The competition consists of two rounds. In the initial online round, applicants will submit abstracts based on the provided problem statements. Shortlisted participants will then advance to the offline hackathon round, where they can build and demonstrate their projects before a distinguished judging panel. IEEE SB RIT hosted the Final Round of PROCOMM 23 on the 2nd of October, 2023.
